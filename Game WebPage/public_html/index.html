You can subscribe to ask us anything or make a recommendation in the "Contact" section or you can register as a user and receive our newsletter so you can access this community and edit the contents of this blog, in addition to being able to access exclusive discounts on all Selected products from the Game store.

  • If you want to be subscribed to our newsletter and register as a user, follow the steps below:
    1. Go to the navigation bar and click on the "User" option, where a menu will be displayed.
    2. Select the "Sign Up" option.
    3. Fill in your personal information.
    4. Check that the option "Receive newsletter" is confirmed and accept the conditions of use by clicking the checkbox
    5. Click Submit button.
  • If you want to contact us and send us an e-mail, follow the steps below:
    1. Go to the navigation bar and click on the "User" option, where a menu will be displayed.
    2. Select the "Contact" option.
    3. Complete the fields.
    4. Click Sent button.
